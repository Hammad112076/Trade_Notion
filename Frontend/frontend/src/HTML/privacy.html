Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use Trade Notion, we collect the following:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Trade data — all trades, notes, P&L figures, and journal entries you log
  • Preferences — settings such as timezone, currency, and notification preferences

We do not collect payment information, browser history, or any data outside of the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the Trade Notion service:

  • To display your trades, analytics, and performance statistics
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To save your preferences and settings

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your trade data is private and visible only to you.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas (cloud database). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. All API requests are authenticated using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export all your trade data at any time (via the Export CSV button in the Trade Journal)
  • Delete individual trades or all trades at once from the platform
  • Request full account deletion by contacting us
  • Update or correct your personal information in Settings

6. Cookies

Trade Notion uses localStorage in your browser to store your authentication token. No tracking cookies or third-party cookies are used.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about your data or this policy, contact us at support@tradenotion.com.
